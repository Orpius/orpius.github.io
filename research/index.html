Distributed Agent Reasoning Across Independent Systems With Strict Data Locality

Daniel Vaughan, Katerina Vaughan
arXiv preprint, November 2025
Multi-agent systems Data locality

The paper presents a proof-of-concept where agents in separate systems cooperate only through natural language messages, while each organisation keeps its own data local behind strict operational boundaries. The focus is on feasibility and architectural patterns rather than clinical or production evaluation.
