About Orpius

Orpius is developed by Outcoder Sàrl, a privately-owned Swiss software company. For more than a decade we have designed and built software for industrial operations around the world. Our background is in delivering systems for supply chain management, automation, and AIDC.

We created Orpius to give development teams the tools and infrastructure needed to integrate LLM systems without taking on unnecessary complexity. With Orpius we focus on practical integration that fits into existing systems, without imposing a framework or requiring teams to build their own AI infrastructure.
