What you need to get started

  1. Orpius Console
    • A lightweight desktop client for configuring everything from models to agents and tools
  2. Access to your own LLM provider(s)
    • Orpius currently supports Google Gemini, OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, with more providers to follow. (Note: Google offers free access to its Gemini API)
