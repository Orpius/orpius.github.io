What you need to get started

  1. Orpius Console
    A lightweight desktop client for configuring models, agents, and tools.

    Orpius connects to leading LLM providers including Google Gemini, OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, Mistral, DeepSeek, and Moonshot Kimi.
    If you are just exploring, Google offers free developer access through the Gemini API .
