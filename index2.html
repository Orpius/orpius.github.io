INFRASTRUCTURE PROTECTION

Orpius features a Secrets Shield and isolated sandboxing. Your passwords and API keys are masked from the LLM and injected only at the moment of execution, while malicious inputs are trapped in a volatile memory silo.

  • → Secrets Shield: Passwords stay hidden from the AI's view.
  • → Zero-Retention: Data is processed in volatile memory and never stored.
  • → Locked Sandbox: Prevents hostile prompts from escaping into your systems.
