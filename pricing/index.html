One powerful platform.

From single-developer setups to fully supported enterprise installations, each tier gives you the full agentic foundation to bring AI into your systems safely and quickly.

Small Business

For individual developers and small teams building with Orpius.

Per month, single developer licence.
  • Full Orpius agentic platform for a single named developer.
  • Use your own models and keys (OpenAI compatible and Gemini compatible models).
  • Allocated storage and compute suitable for individual workloads.
  • Email support with next-business-day response targets and access to documentation and examples.
  • Fully managed updates delivered automatically to your instance.
