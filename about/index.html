About Orpius

Making AI practical for everyday business work. Orpius is built to help individuals and organisations create real business solutions using AI, without needing specialist engineering teams, complex infrastructure, or rigid automation tools. It allows people to describe the work they want done and have AI carry it out safely across their systems.

Orpius is developed by Outcoder SÃ rl, a privately owned Swiss software company. For more than a decade we have designed software for industrial operations around the world, including supply chain automation, warehouse systems, and AIDC solutions.

That experience shaped Orpius. We wanted to make advanced automation accessible to the people who actually run businesses, not just those who build software.

Why Orpius exists

Many AI tools are powerful but difficult to apply to real operational work. Orpius was created to remove that complexity.

  • Build automation by describing outcomes instead of writing code
  • Connect AI safely to your existing systems and processes
  • Run multi-step work reliably over time
  • Keep control of data, models, and infrastructure
  • Scale from individual workflows to organisation-wide automation

Our approach

AI should extend how people work, not replace how businesses operate.

Accessible by design

Orpius is built so people can create automation by describing what they want to achieve, without needing to design workflows or maintain infrastructure.

Built for real operations

Our background is industrial software. Orpius focuses on reliability, supervision, and integration with existing business systems.

Flexible and future-proof

Choose your own models, deployment location, and integrations. Orpius is designed to evolve as AI capabilities grow.
